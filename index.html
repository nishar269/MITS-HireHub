About MITS

Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science (MITS) is a premier engineering college located in Madanapalle, Andhra Pradesh, established in 1998. Affiliated with JNTUA and accredited by NAAC, MITS offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and computer applications. Known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong placement record, MITS is committed to fostering innovation, research, and holistic development of students.

Vision: To be a globally recognized institute for academic excellence and innovation.

Mission: To provide quality education, promote research, and develop skilled professionals to meet industry needs.
